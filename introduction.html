Exam Lab
Introduction

Read the instructions below carefully:

1. Read each question carefully before deciding which option to select. If you are unsure of a question, can you flag it by clicking the ? which is below the question. And at any time you can click on Review Exam and review the highlighted questions.

2. The test has a total of 60 questions and your answers are mandatory to complete the simulation.

3. Pay attention to questions and multiple choices. Each question has a number of answers to be marked.

4. The duration of the simulation is 1 hour and 30 minutes. You can track time using the counter. If time runs out, a warning will appear.

5. It is only possible to complete the exam after completing 60 questions. Then, at the end, click on Review Exam to check if you marked all the answers and once you are ready, click on Finish Exam.

6. At the end, you will have a summary of your performance in this simulation. You will be shown how long it takes to complete the test, how many questions you answered correctly and a percentage of your effort in completing it. Furthermore, in Review Exam you can check which questions you got wrong and check your answers.

7. To start the simulation, click Start.
